France used old tactics and outdated weaponery during the World-War 2. Though the Maginot line was a great defensive idea, they didn't extended it throughout the french borders. Germany capitalized on this weakness and started invading France from Belgium. The French also left the Ardenne forests defenseless, as they thought it was impenetrable. But the Germans attacked through the Ardenne forests with most of their forces. And France fell within weeks.

After France declared war on Germany, they mobilized millions of troops to Belgium and Netherlands to prevent the invasion of France. But the Germans pushed throught the Ardenne forests, encircled the french army and overwhelmed them.

Some of the french troops escaped through dunkirk. The Germans occupied all the coast line of France and kept a German puppet government called "The Vishy France" in the remaining part. It was not untill 1944, after the Normandy landings, that France was liberated. Life in France was horrible after Germany occupied. There were no human rights, people were taken as forced labour, taxes were raisen way too much.
